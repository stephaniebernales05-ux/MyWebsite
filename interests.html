My Interests and Hobbies

In my free time, I enjoy going to concerts and exploring new places as a way to relax. One of my favorite concert experiences has been going to Puerto Rico with my sister and some friends and seeing Rauw Alejandro at el choliseo. This experience was especially special because given that the show was the artist's hometown show, he brought out many special guests and references to his Puerto Rican culture that helped make the whole experiencemore personal. To go along with this experience, visiting Puerto Rico was a really beautiful experience given its scenery and beaches. Another one of my favorite concert experiences has been Coldplay since the whole experience felt very nostalgic. I also really enjoy getting to visit new places, one of my favorites having been Puerto Rico because of its beautiful scenery and beaches. I also really enjoyed visiting Canada, which I was initially hesitant of visiting given that I never felt there was anything I was really interested in visiting in Canada, however, when I was there I quickly changed my mind. Other hobbies I enjoy doing include drawing and reading. From a young age, I developed a strong interest in drawing and painting as a way to express myself creatively. Similarly, reading has always been a strong interest of mine, especially genres like mystery and romance.
